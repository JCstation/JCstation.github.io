Paper PBR

Many types of various plain paper, wrapping paper, maps, cardboards and tissues. Inside you will find 100 textures including:

  • paper sheets
  • crumpled paper
  • cardboards
  • burnt papers
  • old maps
  • postcards
  • book pages
  • book covers
  • paper lanterns
  • paper tissues
  • money bills
  • decorative wrapping papers
  • papyrus
  • paper towels

All textures are available in both 8K and 4K resolutions. Each texture contains eight maps, such as ambient occlusion, diffuse/color, glossiness, height/displacement, metalness, normal, reflection / specular, roughness, and is ready to use in Specular/Glossiness and Metalness/Roughness workflows. Along with the PBR textures maps you will get pre-made materials for: 3ds max + V-Ray, Blender, Unreal Engine, Unity 3D (HDRP) and in SBSAR format.
